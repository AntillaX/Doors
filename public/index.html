    How to Play

    • A puzzle is shown to all players. Discuss together — then vote for who delivers the answer.
    • Correct: deliverer loses 3 points. Advance to the next room.
    • Wrong: deliverer loses 5 points. A new puzzle is drawn and the vote reopens.
    • Reach 0 points and you're eliminated. You stay as a spectator.
    • Table fails if fewer than 3 players remain. 30-minute time limit.
    • 4 players: clear 10 rooms to win.  5 players: clear 11 rooms.
